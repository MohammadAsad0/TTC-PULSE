# TTC Pulse Final Storyline

## One-Sentence Version
TTC Pulse is a reliability observatory combining historical TTC delay data, GTFS structure, and live service alerts to show where disruptions recur, when they recur, why they happen, how severe they become, and whether live alerts align with those same risky routes and stations.

## Best Thesis
TTC unreliability is recurrent, local, explainable, and measurable enough to support targeted operational decisions.

## Presentation Arc
1. Reliability is not one average number.
2. Disruptions recur across specific routes, stations, and time windows.
3. Data is messy; integration/normalization is part of the contribution.
4. Layered pipeline: historical delays + GTFS + live alerts.
5. Reliability pillars: frequency, severity, regularity, cause mix.
6. Hotspots and rankings.
7. Live-alert alignment.
8. Dashboard delivery.
9. Limitations and next steps.
